Experience Spiritual Clarity with NoorSalat

NoorSalat is your dedicated companion for a disciplined and peaceful spiritual life. Designed with simplicity and precision in mind, our app ensures that you stay connected to your prayers (Salah) no matter where you are in the world.

Whether you are at home or traveling, NoorSalat provides the essential tools every Muslim needs to fulfill their daily worship with ease and "Noor" (light).

Key Features:
Precise Prayer Times: Get highly accurate prayer timings based on your current location. Customize notifications so you never miss a Fard prayer.

Qibla Finder: An easy-to-use, accurate compass to help you find the direction of the Kaaba from anywhere in the world.

Daily Adhkar & Tasbih: Strengthen your connection with Allah through a built-in digital Tasbih counter and a collection of authentic daily supplications.

Islamic Calendar: Stay updated with important Islamic dates, including Ramadan, Eid-al-Fitr, and Eid-al-Adha.

Clean & Minimalist Interface: Enjoy a distraction-free experience with a beautiful, modern design that is easy to navigate for all age groups.

Location-Based Accuracy: Automatically detects your city to provide the most reliable local prayer schedule.

Why Choose NoorSalat?
In a busy world, NoorSalat acts as your digital reminder to pause, reflect, and pray. We focus on providing a fast, lightweight, and ad-minimal experience so your focus remains where it matters most: on your Deen.
